🔧 Enhancement Techniques Used
🌙 Night Vision
CLAHE histogram equalisation on LAB L-channel. Gamma curve lift to recover shadow detail.
🌫️ Dehazing
Dark Channel Prior algorithm. Estimates atmospheric light and removes haze/fog from all channels.
🔘 Sharpening
Unsharp masking (Gaussian subtraction). Recovers edge definition lost in camera blur or rain.
🌈 White Balance
Grey-world assumption auto white balance. Corrects colour casts from artificial lighting.
